B.A. B.Ed (Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Education)

B.A. B.Ed. is a four year integrated programme which aims at integrating general studies in arts and professional studies in education. This programme is essentially a timesaving dual-degree programme that enables a student to earn a Bachelors degree in Arts (B.A.) discipline as well as a Bachelors degree in Education (B.Ed.).
